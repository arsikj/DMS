Help

Thank you for using our application. DMS is intended to help diabetics to manage better their diabetes and keep it under control. Although diabetes is not curable, it is a treatable disease and individuals who take a proactive role in their own treatment achieve the best long-term results. One of the critical functions of a proper diabetes management plan is monitoring blood sugar levels. Exercise is also a key component to managing diabetes. In this application, you can input your daily blood glucose and exercise information.

Login Screen

On start up, you can see the login screen where a list of all users is shown (see image below).

*Note: you can only have 9 users at the same time!

On the bottom of the screen there is an ADD button which allows you to insert a new user

Adding new user

On the screen below, we can add a new user. You need to fill all of the fields on the screen in order to successfully add the user. You need to provide your name, SSD (Social Security Number), your doctors name and number (you can also insert them through searching your phone contacts). Finally, there are two fields for your normal glucose range and when you are finished filling this fields you can press on the SAVE button to complete the action.

*Note: if you don't know your normal glucose range you should contact your doctor.

Once you're logged in (by simply clicking on the user list from the login screen) you can continue with inputing your glucose and exercise measurements. By swiping from left to to right you can see the application menu containing all the functionality of the app. As you can see on the image

Glucose monitoring screen

If you select Glucose Monitoring from the menu you can add a new glucose measurement (you can add only one per day). If you haven't input any, the app will remind you to do that. This are the results for different inputs
